EKE JOHN IGWE

Eke John was born and raised in Osun State. He was the third child of his parent who are both Indigenes of Abia State. He started his elementary education in 2001 and finished in 2009. He was enrolled into Courage Comprehensive College in 2009, he was the Laboratory Prefect while in school. He completed his secondary education in 2015 where he passed the senior secondary school certificate examination(SSCE) in one sitting.

John got admitted into The Polytechnic, Ibadan in 2015 to study Mechanical Engineering after being declined admission at University of Ibadan. He graduated in 2017 as the best student in Mechanical Engineering with Distinction. John has worked in different companies in Lagos and Ibadan during his Internship period. He gained admission into the University of Lagos in 2018, where he is pursuing his first degree certificate in Mechanical Engineering.

John developed interest in programming earlier this year and he has been involved in so many hackathons and coding challenge. He is currently an intern student in HNG. He's also a member of University of Lagos Energy Club, Developers Student Club, Unilag Chapter, flutter community, Lagos chapter. John has received certifications in Python language, SQL, HTML and CSS
